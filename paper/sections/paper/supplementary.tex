\subsection{S5: SST Climatology Data}
\label{sec:supp_sst}

Sea surface temperature forcing was derived from the NOAA Optimum
Interpolation SST version~2.1 dataset \citep{oisstv21}, a
0.25\textdegree{} resolution global daily product. For each of the
11~network nodes, day-of-year climatological means were computed from
24~years of monthly data (2002--2025), accessed via the NOAA Physical
Sciences Laboratory OPeNDAP server, and interpolated to daily
resolution.
